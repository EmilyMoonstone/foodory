# Product Vision

## What is Foodory?

**Foodory** (Food + Story) is a social food-sharing app for groups of friends.  
The app combines visual posting like Instagram with structured food journaling and group participation mechanics.

## Core idea

Users share what they cooked (or ate out), tell the story behind it, and interact in friend and group contexts.

## Experience goals

1. **Visual-first:** image-driven feed with clean, modern design.
2. **Social and playful:** friends, groups, reactions, and weekly recaps.
3. **Useful over time:** personal and group history, saved posts, and collaborative cookbooks.
4. **Flexible sharing:** post to all friends, one or multiple groups, or both in one action.
